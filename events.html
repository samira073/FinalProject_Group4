EVENTS

Lollapalooza | Music Festival



Lollapalooza is a Chicago music festival held every August at Grant Park. It offers a diverse lineup of over 100 artists from all types of genres & background. The first ever event was held in 1991 and the tradition has only grew in popularity since. Lollapalooza also sheds light on local food & art vendors. This event proposes great festival atmosphere & allows for attendees to create memorable experiences with the power of music!


ESTIMATED PRICE: ONE DAY - $125

Chicago Marathon | Marathon



The Chicago Marathon is arguably one the greatest marathons in the world. There to attend will be well known runners & enthusiast alongside the cities metropolitans cheering them on. This marathons course wraps around about 29 diverse neighborhoods in a very unique way. Attending gives you the oppurtunity to watch an intense group of runners work to achieve this accolade. Join thousands of others for this unforgettable experience with live music of course!


ESTIMATED PRICE: US RESIDENT - $230

Taste of Chicago | Food Festival



Known to be one of the most jam packed food festivals in the world, Taste of Chicago stands tall for another year. This festival has been a Chicago tradition for over 40 years and offers 70+ different restaurants to sample food from. It runs for about five days in the month of July at Grant Park with live music & a bunch of interactive family activities. The atmosphere this event presents is undeniable & so are its aromas!


ESTIMATED PRICE: FREE ADMISSIONS - COST MAY VERY PER MEAL / ACTIVITY

Chicago Air Show | Air Show



The Chicago Air Show has been around for over 60 years making it one of longest running air shows North America has ever seen. The rich history behind this North Avenue Beach event isn't all, you get to experience the most unique aerial displays by helicopters, fighter jets, stunt planes, and more. Attendees have enjoyed this event annually because it is family-friendly and brings people together to enjoy beyond the air show. There are tons of food vendors, merchant vendors, and interactive activities to keep the fun going. Attend this show & watch the aircrafts fly as high as your dreams!


ESTIMATED PRICE: FREE ADMISSIONS - COST MAY VERY PER MEAL / ACTIVITY

Chicago International Film Festival | Film Festival



The Chicago International Film Festival is one for the books being one of the cities most cherished events. This event offers world-renowned films of all types of genres. Filmmakers and actors also show up to engage with attendees making it so much more surreal. The booked venues are always a site to see with a beautiful essence & architecture.


ESTIMATED PRICE: INDIVIDUAL FILM - $20 | FESTIVAL PASS - $100

Chicago Auto Show | Auto Show



The Chicago Auto Show is the largest auto show held in North America. With over a 1,000 vehicles from multiple different manufacturers, it offers the latest models & the rare finds as well. The show is family-friendly & includes enough activities to bring the kids along to have a good time. Industry professionals attend to give advice on the Do's and Dont's in the automobile niche. The entertainment is limitless with celebrity appearances, live music, giveaways, and so much more. So why wouldn't you be there?
